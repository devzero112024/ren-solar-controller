🔒 Overview

Ren Solar Remote Controller ("the App") is developed and maintained solely by DevZero (Ren). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and what is never collected or shared.

This App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from your device to any server operated by the developer.
✅ Data We Do Not Collect

The developer does not collect, store, or have access to any of the following:

Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
Your device's location or GPS data
Your contacts, call logs, or messages
Photos, files, or camera access
Microphone or audio data
Any analytics or behavioral tracking data
📡 IR Signals and Local Storage

All IR commands (ON, OFF, Blink, Stop) are transmitted locally from your device's built-in IR blaster directly to your solar light. No IR signal data is logged, stored externally, or sent to any server.

Your usage counts (ON uses, Blink uses, total remaining) are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences system. This data never leaves your device and is only used to track your in-app usage balance.

📺 Advertising (Google AdMob)

This App uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements and optional rewarded video ads. AdMob is a third-party advertising service operated by Google LLC.

AdMob may collect and use data from your device — including advertising identifiers, device information, and general usage data — to serve relevant advertisements. This data is collected and processed by Google, not by the developer.

To learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, visit: policies.google.com/privacy
🌐 Internet Access

The App requires internet access for two purposes only:

AdMob ads — to load and display banner and rewarded video advertisements.
Version check — the App fetches a small JSON file from the developer's public GitHub repository to check if a newer version is available. This request contains no personal data and no user identifiers.
⚙ App Permissions
TRANSMIT_IR — required to send IR commands to your solar light via the device's IR blaster.
INTERNET — required for AdMob ads and the version check.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — required by AdMob to check network connectivity before loading ads.
👶 Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. The developer does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this App, please contact the developer immediately so it can be removed.
